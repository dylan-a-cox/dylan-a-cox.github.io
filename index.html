Here is a list of the various research institutions I have worked at:

Idaho National Lab  |  Center for Advanced Energy Studies  |  2022

Developed and implemented a procedure for printing 3D ceramic electrolytes for solid-state lithium ion batteries.

German Aerospace Center  |  Institute for Frontier Materials  |  2023

As part of a team, I worked on the development of a solar sintering machine to manufacture bricks from lunar regolith simulant.  I was responsible for the software development and control hardware integration for ensuring consistent process parameters.

Oregon State University  |  High Altitude Liquid Engine Project  |  2023-Present

Led a 11 person team of undergraduate students on the development of an avionics platform for the control and monitoring of a liquid fueled rocket.  Currently working on a collaborative project developing data acquisition systems for civil engineering outreach and education.

University of Flordia  |  Interdisciplinary Microsystems Group  |  2024-Present

Working on high temperature and harsh environment acoustic instrumentation, including sapphire optical pressure sensors and high temperature packaging for silicon carbide piezoelectric pressures sensors.
